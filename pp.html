Hey! Welcome to Promo Partners.

The basic idea is that you upload a trailer for your podcast, and you play other trailers on your podcast.

Rules:

After uploading your trailer, you must play at least one other trailer on your podcast within twenty episodes. Report violation

Audio Specifications:

In order to keep the content high-quality, we ask for at least the following specifications:
MP3 48kHz, 16-bit, 256KBPS Stereo (Lower specs are fine if it's just speech.)
If the audio quality isn’t up to par, I reserve the right to upload or remove content at my discretion.

Thanks!
