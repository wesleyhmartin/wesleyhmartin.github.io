Computer Training for the Blind

Welcome to Technology Training for the Blind

If you're just starting out and want to get familiar with Windows with NVDA, Android, or Audacity for Windows, I'm here for you. Or you might be familiar with the tech, but you have a problem you just can't solve.

What I Train

  • Android Smartphones and tablets using the TalkBack screen reader
  • Windows 11 using the NVDA Screen Reader
  • Audacity 3 with Windows 10 or 11 using the NVDA Screen Reader

If you'd like, I can also remotely access your machine, so I can demonstrate as I explain. I train using either the telephone or Google Meet.
