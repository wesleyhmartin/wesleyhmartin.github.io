Introducing DEB, the Emoji Describer

It's been about two weeks since I blogged last, but I have a very exciting announcement to share with whoever might be reading this post.

I'm introducing a simple little utility as my official public "debut app." It's called DEB, and it's simply an interactive CLI to look up emojis, and uses Google Gemini to describe them.

In the future, I'm hoping to make a GUI for the app sometime.

How Much?

I'm happy to say: nothing. It's completely free.
Gemini costs are up to you. Gemini offers a free usage limit, which is plenty for everyday use.

When?

When will it be released, you ask?
I'm thrilled to say that the app was released alongside this blog post, and is officially available now.
