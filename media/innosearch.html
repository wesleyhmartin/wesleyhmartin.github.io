Introducing Innosearch’s Brand-New Checkout Flow!

A Smarter, Simpler Checkout Experience

At Innosearch, we've always been committed to providing a seamless and secure experience for our users. Since day one, we've trusted Stripe, a leading payment processing platform, to handle all our transactions, including the checkout page. While this ensured top-tier security, it came with some challenges, particularly in terms of accessibility. For many users, completing a purchase was a frustrating task – and for those with vision impairments, the Stripe UI sometimes displayed Captchas that were impossible to resolve.

✨ Today, we’re beyond excited to unveil a completely redesigned checkout interface! While we continue to rely on Stripe for its robust security technology, our new UI takes accessibility to the next level. Whether you're shopping for products or booking flights, our revamped checkout flow is now easier, faster, and more inclusive than ever.

Here’s how it works – and trust us, it’s as smooth as it gets:

  • After browsing our products or flights, simply head to the Checkout page as usual.
  • On the checkout screen, you’ll have the option to make a quick payment using Link, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Amazon Pay.
  • If you prefer to pay by Credit Card, just click on the button and you’ll be prompted to enter your shipping address (for shopping) or your saved address.
  • Next, enter your credit card information and billing address.
  • And... voila! You’re done!

We’re always striving to deliver the best possible experience for our users. If you run into any issues or have questions, we’re here to help – just drop us a message at info@innosearch.ai.
